New: Mountain View Place - Large Warehouse

By garth koyle
February 3, 2023

New Mountain View warehouses are in a great location 4 min from I-15, 3 min from downtown Chubbuck.  Water, Sewer and Garbage included.  Wifi included.  Private Fenced area for storage is available. Perfect for your business or just to store your toys.  

Location: 12687 Go Way Ave Chubbuck, Idaho

Specifications:

  • Built in 2023
  • Available: September 1, 2023
  • Warehouse Space: ~ 2,250 sqft (50'x50', minus 20'x13' office space)
  • Office Space: ~ 210 sqft (20'x13', minus bathroom)
  • Bathroom Space: ~ 50 sqft (6'x8')
  • Garage Doors: Two, 12' X14' Overhead, One Man Door into Office
  • Electrical: 220v wired in, with 3 phase power available
  • HVAC: Gas unit heater in the shop with electric heat and A/C in the office 
  • Flooring: Concrete in the shop with Luxury Vinyl Tile (LVT) in the office and bathroom
  • Accessibility: ADA, Wheelchair Accessible
  • Parking: 3 parking spaces
  • Outside Storage: 500 square feet, gated/private storage area available for additional charge
  • Terms: $1,800 per month, 24 month contract, etc.  $1500 deposit

Contact: Craig Miller 208-221-9617
